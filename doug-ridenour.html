Doug Ridenour has more than 24 years of experience in environmental / natural resource consulting. He specializes in wetlands consulting — preliminary wetland determinations and delineations, assisting private and public clients with Section 404 permitting, and designing and implementing wetland mitigation projects.

Mr. Ridenour has completed threatened and endangered species investigations, tree surveys, environmental assessments, biological evaluations, and stormwater studies. He has written Wildlife Management Plans and served as an Expert Witness. He has also coordinated hazardous and non-hazardous waste transportation and disposal, and completed Phase I and Phase II Environmental Site Assessments.

A Certified Wildlife Biologist, he attends workshops on wetlands, streams, and U.S. Army Corps of Engineers permitting, and presents wetlands consulting seminars to engineering firms that regularly work with Jones & Ridenour, Inc.
