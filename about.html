About Us / Services

Established in 2003, Jones & Ridenour, Inc. offers natural resource consulting to private and public sector clients, with a primary focus on Texas.

While comfortable working across the southern United States, the firm’s primary focus is Texas — particularly North and East Texas. Offices are located in Arlington and Denison, convenient to the Dallas / Fort Worth Metroplex.

Biologists tailor field and in-house services to each project’s needs, maintain strong relationships with regulatory agencies and partners, and sequence work so evaluations and projects move smoothly from first look through compliance monitoring.
